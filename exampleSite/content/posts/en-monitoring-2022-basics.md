## What to watch
- Latency, errors, saturation, traffic (LETS).
- Events, logs, traces, metrics together.

## Practices
- Alert on symptoms, not causes.
- Keep dashboards boring and readable.

## Tooling
Pick one source of truth; avoid dashboard sprawl.
